Second Chances

Purpose

Children in the foster system, espcially teens, have a very rough start to their lives without much direction of where they can go. Second Chance is about giving them that boost they need, a way to learn valuable skills whilst working towards a college centered future. This is done by rebuilding and fixing up jeeps with a foster teen, and once the jeep is fixed, it is sold and used as scholarship money for the foster teen.
